\begin{multicols}{2}

Throughout most of history and the ancient world, especially as seen in the Old Testament, humans interacted with various gods on a regular basis. Entire nations worshiped them, created idols in their image, and lived in ways to please them. In more modern times, at least here in America, we don't really think of there being various other gods. We know they existed in our history, but we would rarely consider than other individuals would be worshiping these gods from ancient times. What were the gods of old and where have they gone?

The Bible mentions a plethora of old gods:
\begin{quotation}
However every nation continued to make gods of its own, and put them in the shrines on the high places which the Samaritans had made, every nation in the cities where they dwelt. The men of Babylon made Succoth Benoth, the men of Cuth made Nergal, the men of Hamath made Ashima, and the Avites made Nibhaz and Tartak; and the Sepharvites burned their children in fire to Adrammelech and Anammelech, the gods of Sepharvaim. - 2 Kings 17:29-31
\end{quotation}
In other places we have mention of Baal, Dagon, Marduk, Moloch, and so many more.
