The Nature of The Devil And His Demons

- Deceives the whole world
    - Revelation 12:9
    
- Resides on Earth
    - Revelation 12:9 (thrown down to earth)
    - 1 Peter 5:8 (prowls like a lion)
    - Isaiah 14:12 (cut down, fallen from heaven)
    
- From heaven
    - Isaiah 14:12
    - Luke 10:18 (fall like lightning)
    
- Disguised as an Angel of Light
    - 2 Corinthians 11:14
    
- Murderer, liar, father of lies
    - John 8:44
    
- Blinds the minds of the world
    - 2 Corinthians 4:4
    - 1 John 5:19
    
- Ruler of the world
    - John 14:30
    - 1 John 5:19
    
- A Tempter
    - Mathhew 4:3 ("The tempter")
    
- Does not cross God (obeys God)
    - 1 John 5:18-19
    - Matthew 4:10-11
    
- Uses all circumstances to attack
    - Ephesians 6:16
    
- A created being (therefore not omnipotent)
    - Colossians 1:16
    
- Not Omnipresent
    - Job 1:7
    - Job 2:2
    - Matthew 4:11
